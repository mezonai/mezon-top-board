# Managing Your Bot

You can view and edit your bot in “My profile”.

## Edit your Bot
For editing, you can click on the “...” button on the bot card or page.

![Edit bot button examples](/img/edit-bot.png)

Or:

![Edit bot button examples](/img/edit-bot2.png)

> Be sure to read the [Bot Guidelines](./bot-guidelines) to avoid any rule violations.

## Delete your Bot
To delete your bot, click on the “Delete” button.

> **Warning:**
> Once you delete your bot, all associated data—including its listing, statistics, and reviews—will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Make sure you’ve backed up any important information before proceeding.
